## Key Insight: project-starter vs Specific Starters

**project-starter** (what we built) is **adaptable** - it works with any tech stack you throw at it.

**Stack-specific skills** (Phase 1) are **specialized** - they provide deeper, more opinionated scaffolding for stacks you use frequently.

### Why Build Both?

**Option A: Just use project-starter**

- Works for any stack
- More general-purpose
- Good for learning variety

**Option B: Build specific starters too**

- Faster setup for your go-to stacks
- Encode your personal patterns and preferences
- More detailed scaffolding
- Better defaults based on experience

**Recommendation**: Use project-starter for now, then build specific starters as you discover patterns you repeat across projects.
